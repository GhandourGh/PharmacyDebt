Help & Tips

Regular Backups

Export a backup regularly (weekly or monthly) to protect your data. Store backups in a safe location.

Search Features

Use the search functionality to quickly find customers by name, phone, or notes. Enable "Note Search" on the customers page to search within customer notes as well.

Donations

Track donations and apply them to customer debts. Search for donors by name when adding donations, and search for customers when applying donations.

Reports & Analytics

Generate detailed PDF reports and view analytics to track your business performance, customer payment trends, and debt aging.

Data Protection

Always export a backup before making major changes or restoring data. The backup includes all customers, products, debts, payments, and donations.
